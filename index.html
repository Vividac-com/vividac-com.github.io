What is the meaning of Vividac?

Vividac is a combination of "Vivid + AC", symbolizing vibrant energy and connection.

"Vivid": meaning lively, clear, or vibrant, suggests dynamic services, creative content, or intense user experiences.
"AC": stands for "Action" or "Access/Connect" (energy, connection, flow).

In Korean, it's a friendly name, "Vividac" which carries the meaning of creatively connecting and moving with vibrancy.
한국어로는 “비비닥”이라는 친근한 이름으로, 무언가를 창의적으로 연결하고 생동감 있게 움직인다는 의미를 담고 있습니다.
